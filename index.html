01 · About me

A bit about who I am

Beyond the resume, here's the actual human.

I'm a final-year B.Tech Computer Science student at Gayatri Vidya Parishad College of Engineering for Women, Visakhapatnam. My tech journey started with one question — "how does this actually work?" — and that curiosity still hasn't left me.
